About Me

Hi, I'm Femi — a student developer based in the UK with a passion for building clean, functional web experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into real products, and I'm currently working towards studying Computer Science at a top UK university.


I started learning HTML and CSS out of curiosity, and it quickly grew into building full web apps with JavaScript, React, Firebase, and AI APIs. My biggest project so far is RevisionFlow — a free AI-powered revision tool I built solo for UK GCSE and A-Level students, which has real users and is live on the web.


Outside of coding, I volunteer on a church media team handling livestreaming, audio, and visual production. I'm always looking for new things to build and new skills to pick up.
